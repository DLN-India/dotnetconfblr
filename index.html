About The Conference

As a follow-on to the dotnetConf virtual conference, we want to keep the momentum going by hosting a dotnetConf.local event here in Bengaluru, India.

Microsoft, Bangalore DotNet User Group (BDotNet) & DotNet Bangalore Meetup bring the famous dotnetConf online virtual conference to your city. Come and Join us to learn about the latest and greatest of Microsoft Technologies directly from Microsoft folks, Microsoft MVPs, Industry experts and Community rockstars.
